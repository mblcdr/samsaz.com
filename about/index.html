About me

Personal

My name is Hamid Moazzami (/hæmɪd/ - he/him). I was born on October 1992. I am married and have no kids.

Professional

The beginning

I am a passionate mobile app developer who like sot create amazing stuff. My mobile development career started when I was experimenting if I can make an Android app to propose to my now wife (She said yes!).

My first year started getting to know Android platform and basic tools to create Android apps.

Oddrun, the first company

Sooner after I started working at Oddrun Smart Solutions. It was a very small company with about 10 employees. We were responsible of creating apps for other companies. The first of which was (now retired) Headphone app. It was a music streaming app with similarities to Spotify. We had to create our own app because of CopyRight laws being non-existent in Iran. That was our attempt to make listening to music (and also paying for it) easier in Iran.

After some time we decided to create an app of our own. It was a social network called Nazdika. The journey of creating a social network app with dating features was one of a kind. We learned a lot about market, optimizing for monitization, realtime on-demand data delivery and tons of other stuff. Nazdika now has more than 1 million active installs and even more MAUs.

Then came the time that we aquired a Live soccer scores and news app called Footballi. It had about 50K installs when we did. We worked on it for some time and with all the knowledge and experience of before, now Footballi has more than 1 million active installs.

The migration to Germany

Fast forward to 2019, the living situation in Iran was getting worse everyday. And although it was hard for me to leave the company that I helped getting shaped, it was time for starting a new journey. So I decided to migrate to another country.
